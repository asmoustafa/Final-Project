Let's get personal!

Welcome, I'm Aafaf!

Aside from being a student, I love maintaining an active and creative lifestyle!

Weightlifting

In highschool, post five years of soccer, I found myself wanting a new outlet, so I began weightlifting! Though inconsistent in the beginning, at fifteen I quickly fell in love with the sport and found myself committed to it. Freshman year of college, I found the Umich Olympic Weightlifting team, though I was new to the movements, lifting in a collective space with Olympic level Coach Robert Morris was an amazing experience!

Covid ArtWork

During the Covid-19 lockdown, after seeing so many make productive use of their time, I decided I wanted to learn a new skill. I grew up always wanting to learn how to draw, but I doubled down on it during the pandemic. I started with sketches of flowers and simple line art, than quickly started drawing portraits. I had fun trying out glass painting as well, and made some as gifts. Since then, I've continued to draw portraits whenever I have the time!
